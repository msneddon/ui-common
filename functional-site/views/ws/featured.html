Featured Narratives

These Featured Narratives demonstrate some of the types of systems biology questions that can be explored in KBase. The datasets, analyses, and thought processes underlying these computational experiments are captured in the form of an interactive reproducible publication called a Narrative. Click on one of the Featured Narratives to open a read-only version that has already been populated with a summary, input datasets, and output from KBase services that have been run on the input data.

You can run the steps in these Narratives yourself, or even change them, by making your own copy. Sign in (if you haven't already) and click the “Copy” button that appears when you hover over a Narrative title. This will let you copy the workspace that the Narrative belongs to. Once you’ve copied the workspace, locate your copy of the Featured Narrative in the resulting list and click to open it. More instructions for copying and working with Narratives can be found here.
